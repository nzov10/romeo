FIGHTING FOR A BETTER, SAFER, MORE AFFORDABLE DISTRICT 28

MEET ROMEO

ROMEO HITLALL’S DEEP ROOTS IN QUEENS ARE THE FOUNDATION OF HIS UNWAVERING COMMITMENT TO HIS COMMUNITY. HE GREW UP HERE, BUILT HIS FAMILY, AND RAISED TWO CHILDREN IN THE HEART OF THE BOROUGH. FOR OVER 25 YEARS, ROMEO HAS BEEN A DEDICATED COMMUNITY LEADER—ACTIVE AS A BUSINESS OWNER IN SOUTH OZONE PARK, THE PRESIDENT OF THE BROOKLYN & QUEENS LIONS CLUB, AND A PASSIONATE ADVOCATE FOR THE RESIDENTS OF QUEENS AND NEW YORK CITY.

AS DISTRICT GOVERNOR OF THE BROOKLYN & QUEENS LIONS CLUB, ROMEO LEADS AN INTERNATIONAL SERVICE ORGANIZATION THAT FOCUSES ON IMPROVING THE HEALTH, WELL-BEING, AND UNITY OF LOCAL COMMUNITIES. UNDER HIS LEADERSHIP, THE LIONS CLUB HAS BROUGHT PEOPLE TOGETHER FOR MEANINGFUL EVENTS LIKE THE ANNUAL CHRISTMAS TREE LIGHTING IN RICHMOND HILL, BACK-TO-SCHOOL GIVEAWAYS, AND CRITICAL COMMUNITY OUTREACH EFFORTS SUCH AS FEEDING FAMILIES IN NEED DURING DISASTERS LIKE HURRICANE SANDY AND THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC.

ROMEO’S IMPACT REACHES FAR BEYOND THE LIONS CLUB. HE HAS COLLABORATED WITH ELECTED OFFICIALS AND COMMUNITY ADVOCATES TO PUSH FOR CLEANER STREETS, MORE GREEN SPACES, AND SAFER NEIGHBORHOODS. HIS WORK AGAINST VIOLENCE HAS EARNED RECOGNITION FROM LOCAL LAW ENFORCEMENT, AND HE WAS INSTRUMENTAL IN THE CREATION OF THE “LITTLE GUYANA” SIGN AT THE OZONE PARK-LEFFERTS BLVD TRAIN STATION. ROMEO ALSO PROUDLY SERVES IN THE NYPD’S COMMUNITY PARTNERS PROGRAM AND HAS BEEN AN ACTIVE MEMBER OF QUEENS COMMUNITY BOARD 10 FOR 17 YEARS.

IN ADDITION TO HIS COMMUNITY WORK, ROMEO IS THE CEO AND OWNER OF NMCRA CONNECTORS REALTY IN SOUTH OZONE PARK. A REAL ESTATE BROKER SINCE 2000, ROMEO’S BUSINESS ACUMEN IS JUST AS STRONG AS HIS COMMITMENT TO THE PEOPLE OF QUEENS.

KEY ISSUES

PUBLIC SAFETY

Romeo believes that safety is a fundamental right for all residents. He is committed to working with local law enforcement, community organizations, and neighborhood groups to create safer streets, improve emergency response times, and ensure our communities are protected from crime.

AFFORDABILITY

Romeo will fight to make sure that District 28 remains affordable for families and seniors. He will work to increase the supply of affordable housing, while making sure to consider the concerns of the community.

ECONOMIC GROWTH

Romeo advocates for sustainable economic growth that benefits everyone. He will work to bring more jobs to District 28, support small businesses, and ensure that economic development focuses on both long-term prosperity and community wellbeing.

QUALITY OF LIFE

Romeo is focused on cleaning up the commercial corridors of Liberty Avenue, Lefferts Boulevard, Baisley Boulevard, Guy Brewer Boulevard, and Rockaway Boulevard, which are plagued by sanitation issues. Romeo will work with the DSNY and NYPD to address these problems by issuing summonses to illegal vendors and personally overseeing cleanup efforts in the community.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Romeo believes in the power of community involvement. He will establish regular town hall meetings and outreach programs to ensure that every voice in District 28 is heard and that citizens have a say in the future of their neighborhood.

“OUR NEIGHBORHOODS NEEDS A REPRESENTATIVE WITH DEEP COMMUNITY TIES, A TRACK RECORD OF SERVING THE COMMUNITY, AND A BOLD VISION FOR QUEENS AND NEW YORK CITY. THAT’S WHY I’M RUNNING FOR CITY COUNCIL – TO SERVE THE 28TH DISTRICT AND THE COMMUNITY THAT RAISED ME” - ROMEO
